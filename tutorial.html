EasieR
Tutorial for first-timers
Introduction
EasieR is written out of frustation for the lack of convinient tools for creating ER Diagrams as taught in an introduction to database module in Nanyang Technological University. This tutorial will teach you how to quickly benefit from this tool.
Starting - Creating a new diagram
Start by clicking "Create a New Diagram" on the EasieR Homepage. It will open a new canvas for work.
Resizing the Canvas
You can resize the canvas by clicking and dragging on the bottom right-hand corner of the canvas.
E - Adding an Entity
On the left-hand menubar, choose "Ent" (or press E). A form will show up. Fill in the entity name, check on the checkbox if the entity is weak entity, and finally press OK. Move your mouse to desired location on the canvas, and finally left-click to place the entity.
W - Toggle the weak property of an entity
Click on the entity for which you want to change its weak property. Then on the left-hand menubar, choose "Wk" (or press W).
A - Adding an Attribute
Click on the entity / relation for which you want to add the new attribute to. Then on the left-hand menubar, choose "Attr" (or press A). Fill in the name of the attribute, and check on the checkbox if the attribute is key.
K - Toggle the key property of an attribute
Click on the attribute for which you want to change its key attribute. Then on the left-hand menubar, choose "Key" (or press K).
R - Adding a Relation
On the left-hand menubar, choose "Rel" (or press R). Fill in the name of the relation, and press OK. Left-click to place the relation on the canvas.
- - Adding an association
Click on a relation for which you want to add the association. Then, on the left-hand menubar, choose "--" (or press -). Choose an entity for which the association will be made. Finally, choose on the type of assocation. The options are "N-" (Many), "<-" (One), and "(-" (Referential Integrity).
I - Adding an Isa Relation
On the left-hand menubar, choose "Isa" (or press I). Choose the parent entity, and then choose the child entity. The Isa triangle will be added automatically.
L - Adding a label for roles on associations
On the left-hand menubar, choose "Lbl" (or press L). Add the caption to the label, and place it to the canvas.
X - Removing an association from a relation
Click on the relation for which you want to remove an association from. On the left-hand menubar, choose "-X-" (or press X). A list of associations that the relation has will be displayed. Click on the association you wish to remove.
D - Deleting an element from the diagram
Click on the element for which you want to delete from the diagram. On the left-hand menubar, choose "Del" (or press D).
N - Renaming a caption
Choose on the element / label for which you want to rename. On the left-hand menubar, choose "reNm" (or press N). Enter the new caption you desire.
G - Saving the Image of the Diagram in PNG file
On the left-hand menubar, choose "Get" (or press G). Enter the name of the diagram, and click on the download link to save your ER diagram in a PNG file.
Moving an Element / a group of elements
Click on an element or select on the group of elements you wish to reposition. Click on the highlighted elements and drag them to the desired location.
